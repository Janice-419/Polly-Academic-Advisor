PolyU Academic Advising RAG Project

Data Sources
------------
- PolyU Student Handbook
- GUR Guidelines
- Subject Registration pages
- Programme & Curriculum pages
- GPA & Academic Performance pages
- Graduation pages
- WIE pages

Pipeline
--------
1. Extract text from PDFs.
2. Extract text from websites.
3. Combine text into polyu_kb.txt.
4. Split text into chunks.
5. Generate embeddings using all-MiniLM-L6-v2.
6. Store embeddings in FAISS.
7. Convert user question into embedding.
8. Retrieve top 3 chunks.
9. Combine chunks into last_context.txt.
